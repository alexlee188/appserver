Nursing - Services

Mobile Nursing is a coordination platform that helps client to engage the services of a private registered nurse providing home care nursing services for patient in the comfort of their residential home.

Mobile Nursing nurses assist patients by providing services like wound care, tube care, injection, monitoring of blood pressure and sugar levels, etc.


Mobile Nursing services includes :

Wound Care

  • Dressing
  • Removal of stitches

Tube Care

  • Insertion / Changing of urinary catheter
  • Insertion / Changing of nasogastric / feeding tubes

Others

  • Injection
  • Checking / Monitoring of Blood Pressure or Sugar Level

Non-Medical

  • Ensuring safety of elderly prone to falling
  • Medication reminder
  • Provide companionship during the day
