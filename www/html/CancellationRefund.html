Cancellation & Refund Policy

Payment in full is required when you make a booking.  This will be refunded in full if you cancel the booking 24 hours before the start of the home visit appointment time.  If you cancel within 24 hours, there is a cancellation charge of $20.

If there is a no show of the Nurse more than 30 minutes after the home visit appointment time, there will be a full refund.
