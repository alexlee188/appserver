Terms of Services

1. Registration
Nurses can register with Mobile nursing if you meet all the criteria as stipulated in our Terms of Services.

2. Registration Fee
There will be no registration fee for Nurses.

3. Eligibility
3.1. Nursing License
Nurses must be a registered nurse with Singapore Nursing Board (SNB) as a licensed practising nurse in Singapore. You must ensure your nursing license is valid throughout the whole period of job assignment with Mobile Nursing.

3.2. Professional Indemnity Insurance
In additional to 3.1. We strongly encourage you to have a valid a professional indemnity insurance coverage.

4. Assignment
Mobile nursing reserves all rights to assign home care nursing jobs as deem relevant to patient's requirement.

5. Payment
Client will credit full payment to Mobile nursing via direct Interbank Funds transfer to DBS account. Full payment will be refund in the event of any cancellation of assignment or "No show" from our nurses.

6. Cancellation of Assignment
After an accepted assignment, if however the nurse decides to cancel the assignment before the first duty, you are obliged to inform Mobile nursing via phone call (65) 97851440 with minimum advance notice of 48 hours before the first duty commences.

Failing to notify or with insufficient notice, an administrative charge of SGD 100 will be imposed.

7. Termination of Assignment
Nurses are highly encouraged to commit to assignment given. Failing to do so may result with penalty charges of SGD 100.

8. Verification of Qualifications
All licensed nurses are registered with Singapore Nursing Board (SNB). You can verify nurse license at SNB website : Singapore Nursing Board

9. Disclaimer
Mobile nursing will not be responsible for resolving any conflict or disagreement that may arise between patient/ patient's family and nurse.

In additional, Mobile Nursing will not be responsible for any improper, negligent practice; misconduct or misuse by the assigned nurse.

Please review and verify all necessary documents and certificates of the registered nurse prior to any assignment rendered.
