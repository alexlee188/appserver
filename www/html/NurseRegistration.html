Nurse Registration

Describes how Nurse can register using SGnurse app.

Dear Nurse,

Thank you for your interest to join Mobile nursing.

Only applicants who are Singapore Citizen, Permanent Resident and/ or who hold a valid working pass will be eligible.

All licensed nurses registered with the Singapore Nursing Board (SNB) are welcome to register with Mobile nursing.

Before submitting the nurse registration form online, please read the Terms of Service carefully. Nurses who sign up with Mobile Nursing is deemed agreeable to our Terms of Service. Particularly of great importance, we regularly conduct routine verification of nurses' license with SNB. Please ensure you maintain your nursing license throughout the course of job placement.

To enrol with Mobile nursing, you must fill in the online registration form in FULL and submit.

Upon receiving your completed application form, we will begin the home care nursing assignment process. You will be contacted either through phone or SMS. So, make sure you keep your registered number accessible. We will be in contact with you very soon.
